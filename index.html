Features

Adjustable Speed

Set text flow speed according to your reading pace. Precise control from 0-500 px/s range.

Customizable Appearance

Font size, color and style options. Customize the prompter to your preference.

Mirror Mode

Mirror view for prompter glass usage. Ideal for professional video shoots.

File Support

Upload TXT files, paste from clipboard or type directly. Multiple input options.

Multi-Direction Support

Vertical and horizontal scrolling options. Advanced controls in desktop version.

Hidden Mode

Hide controls during shooting. Minimal interface for full focus.

Manual Position (Mobile)

Manually adjust text to desired position with touch. Precise control.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Space: Play/Pause, F: Fullscreen, V/H: Change mode and more.

Manual Platform Selection

Recognizes your device and makes suggestions, but the final choice is yours.

How to Use

Sidebar Ad Space
1

Choose the Right Platform

The system recognizes your device and makes suggestions, but the final choice is yours. You can manually select the platform you prefer.

2

Prepare Your Text

Upload a TXT file, paste from clipboard or type directly. One-touch clipboard paste feature on mobile.

3

Optimize Settings

Adjust speed, font size, color and appearance settings to your preference. Shoot professionally with mirror mode.

4

Start Your Shoot

Press play or use spacebar. Hide controls with hidden mode for full focus.

Pro Tips

📱 Mobile Tips

  • Single touch to play/pause
  • Horizontal swipe to open/close controls
  • Touch for manual position adjustment
  • H key for hidden mode

🖥️ Desktop Tips

  • F11 for fullscreen mode
  • V/H keys to change mode
  • Ctrl+D for desktop shortcut
  • R key to quickly reset
